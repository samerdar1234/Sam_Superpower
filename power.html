The Beginnings...

Not just your average superhero

Now this is a story all about how, statistics got flipped turned upside down. I'd like to take a minute, just sit right there. I'll tell you how I became the outlier of Saline.

Walking down the aisle of Walmart's parking lot, I could feel the hush of normalicy cross the sky. As if fate had been determined, a radioactive flying book came shooting towards me. I tried to run. The bell curves overtook me though, and I couldn't escape the statistical chance that I had to become the owner of all opportunities. I blacked out from the blow of that book. When I woke up, the radioactive statistics book was gone, but the ability to point and change the likelihood of anything happening was left behind.


Powerhouse

The concept of my powers is this: I can control the probability of things happening.


Problem Examples My Solutions
Oh no! There's a test on HTML that I didn't study for! Change the likelihood of my guessing an answer correctly to have an 80% chance of happening.
Oh no! My code is buggy! Change it so I have a 50% chance of finding the bug every consecutive minute I continue to work on it

Too much of a good thing...

For all superheros, there is a limit. Nothing in life has a completely determined outcome and there is nothing I can do to make fate certain. I can range the probability of things happening between 20%-80%. There is always the likihood that things don't turn out how I plan or things only turn out 80% how I want, but that's where the human portion of me adapts and overcomes.

Another unfortunate thing is that if an outcome already had a 0.01% chance of happening, I may have altered it to have a 20% chance of happening instead. Beware friends, the likihood of getting killed by a cow is low, but never zero...
